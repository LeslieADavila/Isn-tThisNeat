Short Synopsis

My name is Leslie Davila. I am 23 and a graduate assistant in the Department of History at Texas A&M University. I am currently in my second year of the master’s program. Inspired by scholars who came before me and the continued need to address the long ignored Mexican victims of murderous violence, I study racial violence in 19th century Texas. Like other academics, I have a long list of interests that do not stop at what I study. I consider myself an active millennial, so I can and am often found on Twitter and other areas of the internet. As such, the disclaimer that all thoughts there and here are my own and do not necessarily reflect the ideals of the university that employs me.

She/her pronouns.
